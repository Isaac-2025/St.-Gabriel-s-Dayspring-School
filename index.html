Who We Are

Nurturing Minds, Building Character

Founded with a passion for excellence, St. Gabriel's Dayspring School is a premier basic educational institution in Ghana. We are dedicated to providing a holistic education that combines academic rigor with moral discipline.

Our mission is to create a safe, stimulating environment where every child is encouraged to explore their potential and blossom into responsible future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of academic and moral excellence.

Our Values

Discipline, Integrity, Excellence, and Faith.

"We believe every child is a unique gift capable of greatness."

- The Proprietress

Academic Excellence

Levels of Learning

We follow the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum, enriched with Montessori principles to ensure practical understanding from the very start.

Creche & Nursery

A safe, loving "home away from home" where infants and toddlers develop motor skills through sensory play.

  • Expert Caregivers
  • Safe Environment

Kindergarten (KG)

Building the foundation for literacy and numeracy through creative activities and Montessori methods.

  • Phonics & Reading
  • Creative Arts

Primary School

A rigorous curriculum covering Science, Math, English, and ICT to foster critical thinking and academic excellence.

  • ICT & Computing
  • Sports & Culture
